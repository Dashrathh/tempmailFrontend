## The Problem with Using Your Personal Email

When you sign up for a free trial on a major streaming service, you are trading your personal email address for 7-30 days of access. Here is the problem with that trade:

1. **You Can Only Claim it Once:** Platforms track your main email address. Once it's used, that's it—no more free trials for you.
2. **Endless Marketing Spam:** Streaming companies are notorious for sending relentless "We miss you!" and "Upgrade Now!" emails months, or even years, after your trial ends.
3. **Data Logging:** Your email becomes permanently tied to a user profile, your taste in movies, and your demographic data, which is often shared or sold to third-party advertising networks.
